Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order?

    Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, and checkout to place your order. Our staff will then prepare your order and serve it to you at your table.

  • How long does it take to receive my order?

    We strive to serve your order as quickly as possible. Our staff will let you know when your order is ready and will bring it to your table.

  • Can I request special instructions for my order?

    Yes, you can request special instructions for your order, such as no MSG or gluten-free. Please let our staff know when you place your order.

Account and Login

  • How do I reset my password?

    Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

  • How do I update my account information?

    Log in to your account and click on the "Account" link to update your information.
